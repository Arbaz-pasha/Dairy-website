ABOUT US

Vision and Mission


Vision

To march forward with a missionary zeal which will make BMP a trailblazer of exemplary performance and achievements beckoning other Milk Federations in the country in pursuit of total emulation of its good deeds. To ensure prosperity of the rural Milk producers who are ultimate owners of the Federation. To promote producer oriented viable cooperative society to impart an impetus to the rural income, dairy productivity and rural employment. To abridge the gap between price of milk procurement and sale price. To develop business acumen in marketing and trading disciplines so as to serve consumers with quality milk, give a fillip to the income of milk producers. To compete with MNCs and Private Dairies with better quality of milk and milk products and in the process sustain invincibility of cooperatives.

Mission

Heralding economic, social and cultural prosperity in the lives of our milk producer members by promoting vibrant, self-sustaining and holistic cooperative dairy development in Karnataka State. The mission of the mission Diligently forging the Karnataka Milk Federation into an exemplary organization in the country with exemplary objectives and good thinking, and to be a role model for other milk superintendents with unique achievements. To make rural milk producers the backbone of Karnataka Milk Federation. To foster a co-operative society that encourages rural employment, dairy production and income generation and amends the interests of milk producers. Reducing the gap between milk sales and milk storage rates. Incorporate high value intensive business activity in order to provide high quality milk to the consumers and motivate the milk producer's income. Providing good quality milk and milk products while maintaining healthy competition with private dairies and multinationals, maintaining good co-operation.
Solidarity
Banglore Milk production aims to create economic, social and cultural prosperity in the lives of our member milk producers by implementing an effective, self-reliant and fully integrated dairy development program in the state of Karnataka.
